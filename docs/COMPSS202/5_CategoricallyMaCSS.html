5 | We are Predicting Data (with a Categorical IV)

TipObjectives This Week
  • Define and interpret linear models using R to make better predictions than the mean alone…when the IV is a categorical variable.
  • Define and interpret \(R^2\) to evaluate the amount of error in our predictions…when the IV is a categorical variable.
  • Learn to re-level the categorical variable to (potentially) aid in the interpretation of the slope(s).
