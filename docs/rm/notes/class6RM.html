Please submit answers to the graded Canvas discussion post to help me keep track of your work, but when prompted please also copy / paste your work to our Vision Board so we can more easily see (and expand upon) this work together in class.

  1. IN CLASS. We counted the number of interruptions in a video before and after operationalizing an interruption.   3. ON THE VISION BOARD. Look over the list of personality surveys from the IPIP Website. Do you think this is a (face) valid way to measure the variable? Why / why not?
