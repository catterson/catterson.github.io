Readings for Next Week (and Quiz 4)

  1. Read through the chapter on Observational Research. Focus on the different types of observational studies and the hawthorne effect.

  2. Read through the chapter on the Specific Affect Coding System (SPAFF). Focus on the “three rules” of people watching, and the different guidelines for behavioral coding (e.g., function, indicator, cues, counter-indicators). You can skim the stuff on “Action Units” in the face, though note this is an example of how psychologists have tried to quantify (and operationalize) emotion.
