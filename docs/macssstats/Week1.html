Lab 1. Step Problems

Answer the questions below as a .PDF; make sure your code is visible alongside your output. Please make sure to adhere to the course AI policy, and please ask for help if you get stuck / are confused / professor made a mistake.

Access the two “Steps and BMI” data files There are two data files - data9b_w.txt reports the Body Mass Index (bmi) and the number of steps taken in a day (steps) for a sample of women, and data9b_m.txt reports these same variables for a sample of men.

  1. In class, we worked through terms of validity and reliability. Apply these terms to think about how you might evaluate the steps data and BMI data in the dataset. (You don’t need to do any stats for this question.)
Term Steps Data BMI Data
Face validity
Convergent Validity
Discriminant Validity
Test-Retest Reliability
Interjudge Reliability
  1. What is the relationship between the number of steps that people take and their BMI a) for all participants, b) for women, and c) for men? Report the relevant statistics you need to answer these questions. There are different ways to approach this - so just do your best to answer the question and show your work and you’ll get full credit.
